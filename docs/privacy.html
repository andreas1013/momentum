Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Momentum ("we", "our", or "us") is a habit tracking app built by Andreas. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Your email address, used to create and manage your account
  • Habit names and daily habit logs you create in the app
  • Basic usage data to improve the app

What we do not collect

  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not share your data with third parties for advertising
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or photos

How your data is stored

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted in Singapore. Your habit data is private to your account and is not visible to other users.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use them only to send habit reminders and recovery prompts. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Deleting your data

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process requests within 30 days.

Children

Momentum is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@momentum-app.com or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
